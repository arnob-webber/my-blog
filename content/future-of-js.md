# The Future of JavaScript in 2026

JavaScript continues to evolve at a rapid pace. In 2026, we're seeing a massive shift towards **edge computing** and **enhanced developer experience**.

## Key Trends

1.  **AI-Integrated Frameworks**: Frameworks are now coming with built-in AI hooks for better optimization.
2.  **WebAssembly Everywhere**: Performance critical parts of our apps are moving to Wasm.
3.  **Unified State Management**: Finally, we have a standard for state across frameworks.

> "The only constant in software development is change, especially in JavaScript."

Stay ahead of the curve!
