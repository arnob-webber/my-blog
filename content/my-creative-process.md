# Behind the Scenes: My Creative Process

Every piece starts with a spark of inspiration. Today, I want to share the steps I take to create my digital art.

## 1. The Sketching Phase
I always start with a very rough sketch. It’s not about being perfect; it's about getting the **composition** right. 

![The initial sketch](https://imgeng.jagran.com/images/2025/08/07/article/image/kiara-advani-bikini-body-secrets-1754587775969.webp)

## 2. Choosing the Palette
Color is everything. For this piece, I wanted to focus on:
* **Warm oranges** for the highlights.
* **Deep purples** for the shadows.
* A touch of teal for contrast.

## 3. Final Details
The last 10% of the work takes 50% of the time! Adding highlights and texture is what brings the piece to life.

> "Art is never finished, only abandoned." – Leonardo da Vinci
